Safety Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Do not share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PINs with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank. (UPI Safety)
Always verify payment requests before approving them on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe. (UPI Safety)
Avoid clicking on unknown links received via SMS, email, or social media—they may be phishing attempts.
Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
Check website security (HTTPS) before entering personal or financial details.
Log out from shared/public computers and avoid saving passwords on them.
Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi—avoid accessing banking or sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
Regularly update your phone and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities.
Think before sharing on social media—limit personal information to prevent identity theft.
