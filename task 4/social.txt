Do’s
Think before you post – ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Protect your privacy – adjust privacy settings and limit personal information sharing.
Verify information before sharing – check sources to avoid spreading misinformation.
Maintain professionalism – remember that future employers may view your profiles.
Engage positively – support and encourage others, and contribute to meaningful discussions.


Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive personal information like your address, phone number, or passwords.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling – avoid harmful or offensive comments.
Don’t post inappropriate content (offensive language, explicit images, etc.).
Don’t fall for suspicious links or scams – be cautious of phishing attempts.
Don’t overshare – avoid posting every detail of your personal life publicly.
